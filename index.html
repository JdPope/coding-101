Crunchy Granola Pretzel Sticks

Ingredients

  • 1 package of chocolate chips
  • 24 pretzel rods
  • 1 cup granola without raisins

Instructions

  1. Melt chocolate chips in microwave.
  2. Pour into one side of a large shallow dish.
  3. Roll each pretzel halfway into chocolate.
  4. Sprinkle pretzels with granola.
  5. Place on waxed paper until set.
